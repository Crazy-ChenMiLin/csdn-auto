## 2026-05-20 学习进度

### ✅ 已掌握知识点

#### 1. 三大从句（语法基础）
- **定语从句**：修饰名词，找先行词
- **名词性从句**：主语从句、宾语从句、表语从句
- **状语从句**：表时间、原因、条件等

#### 2. 长难句拆解三步法
```
第一步：找主干（主谓宾）
第二步：划修饰（从句、介词短语）
第三步：梳理逻辑（谁修饰谁）
```

#### 3. 限制性 vs 非限制性定语从句
| 类型 | 标志 | 作用 |
|------|------|------|
| 限制性 | that（无逗号） | 必要信息 |
| 非限制性 | which（有逗号） | 补充信息 |

#### 4. 形式主语结构
- `It is widely believed that...` = 人们普遍认为...
- It 是形式主语，真正主语在 that 后面

### 📝 练习过的长难句

1. The research which was conducted by scientists from Harvard University suggests that regular exercise can significantly reduce the risk of heart disease.
   - 主干：The research suggests that...
   - 修饰：which...（修饰 research）

2. The new policy that was announced by the government yesterday, which aims to reduce carbon emissions, has sparked heated debates.
   - 主干：The new policy has sparked heated debates
   - 修饰：that... 和 which... 都修饰 policy

3. It is widely believed that the increasing popularity of online shopping, which has been driven by..., has contributed to the decline of traditional retail stores that have failed to adapt...
   - 主干：It is believed that... popularity has contributed to the decline
   - 修饰：which...（修饰 popularity），that...（修饰 stores）
